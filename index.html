// The Problem

A Silent Threat in Plain Sight

Singapore faces a worsening cardiac crisis — and the most at-risk individuals are often the least equipped to detect it.

Cardiovascular Disease Crisis

Cardiovascular disease accounted for over 30% of all deaths in Singapore in 2024 — a persistent, leading cause of mortality that demands better early detection tools.

Projected Triple by 2050

Research projects that heart attack incidence could triple by 2050, driven by aging populations, rising obesity rates, and increasingly sedentary work culture.

The Detection Gap

Sedentary individuals resuming exercise often carry hidden risks of silent ischemia — reduced blood flow to the heart that standard fitness trackers and smartwatches simply cannot detect.

The Timeline of Risk

Today: 2024 30%+ CVD mortality rate in Singapore. Ischemia often goes undetected in active-onset individuals.
2030–2040: Rising Burden Aging population + "sudden active" demographic surge increases cardiac event risk.
2050: Projected Crisis 3× increase in heart attack incidence without improved early-detection infrastructure.
Silent Ischemia: Reduced cardiac blood flow that produces no symptoms — undetectable without clinical-grade ECG signal analysis. Smartwatches measure heart rate; our system measures heart health.
// Our Solution

The Wearable ECG System

A clinical-grade wearable that compares your resting baseline against your active stress-state — in real time, during exercise.

ADS1298 · nRF52840 · BLE 5.0
Transmitting via BLE
Active RLD Circuit
24-bit Resolution · Ultra-low Power
01

Reduced-Lead Strategy

Unlike multi-lead hospital ECGs, our system uses a targeted reduced-lead approach optimised for wearability without sacrificing diagnostic sensitivity for ischemia detection.

02

Baseline vs. Stress Comparison

Captures a resting ECG baseline before exercise, then continuously compares active signals against it to identify ischemic biomarkers that emerge under cardiac stress — your body's own reference point.

03

Sports-Oriented Design

Engineered for the transition from sedentary to active — the highest-risk window for cardiac events. Designed to be worn during workouts, not just at rest.

04

Cloud-Backed Intelligence

Heavy signal processing offloaded to a cloud backend via RESTful API, keeping the mobile interface lightweight and responsive for real-time feedback.

// Technical Architecture

Engineering Precision,
Layer by Layer

A carefully co-designed hardware and software stack built for clinical-grade signal fidelity in a wearable form factor.

Column A · Hardware Engineering

The Physical Layer

ADS1298 AFE

High-resolution 24-bit analog front-end for ECG acquisition. Industry-standard chip designed specifically for biopotential measurement with exceptional signal-to-noise ratio.

nRF52840 MCU

Ultra-low power microcontroller with native Bluetooth Low Energy 5.0 support. Enables sustained wearable operation without compromising wireless data transmission fidelity.

Active RLD Circuit

Active Right Leg Drive circuit provides advanced noise cancellation and common-mode rejection — eliminating environmental electrical interference that would corrupt ECG signals during movement.

Column B · Software & Data Pipeline

The Intelligence Layer

NeuroKit2 · Python

A Python-based processing pipeline leveraging NeuroKit2 for automated signal cleaning, R-peak detection, and heart rate variability extraction — transforming raw voltages into clinical metrics.

SQI Algorithm

Signal Quality Indexing via a custom sliding-window algorithm. Kurtosis (kSQI) and Morphological (mSQI) metrics work in tandem to autonomously accept high-quality data windows and reject noise contamination.

RESTful Cloud API

Heavy computational processing is offloaded to a cloud backend via a RESTful API architecture, keeping the mobile user interface lightweight and enabling real-time streaming feedback.
